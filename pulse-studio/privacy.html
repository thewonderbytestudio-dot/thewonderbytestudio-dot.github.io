Privacy Policy for Pulse Studio

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

Pulse Studio is a haptic pattern app that lets users create, save, and play custom vibration patterns on their device.

This Privacy Policy explains how Pulse Studio handles information.

Developer and Contact Information

Developer: Wonderbyte Studio
Contact: thewonderbytestudio@gmail.com

Information We Collect

The current version of Pulse Studio does not require an account and does not ask users to provide personal information.

The current version of Pulse Studio does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Local App Data

Pulse Studio stores certain app data locally on your device so the app can function properly. This may include:

  • Saved vibration patterns
  • Pattern names
  • Favorite pattern status
  • Session length preference
  • Safety acknowledgement status

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to Pulse Studio servers.

Vibration Permission

Pulse Studio uses your device’s vibration feature to play haptic patterns.

The vibration permission is used only to support app features such as preset patterns, custom patterns, and saved pattern playback.

Accounts, Cloud Storage, Analytics, and Advertising

The current version of Pulse Studio does not require user accounts.

The current version of Pulse Studio does not use cloud storage or cloud sync.

The current version of Pulse Studio does not use analytics services.

The current version of Pulse Studio does not use advertising services.

If accounts, cloud features, analytics, advertising, or other third-party services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what data is collected, used, or shared.

Data Sharing

Pulse Studio does not sell personal information.

The current version of Pulse Studio does not share personal information with third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

Locally saved app data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, clear the app’s data, or uninstall the app.

Pulse Studio does not currently store user data on external servers.

Data Security

Pulse Studio does not transmit saved pattern data to external servers in the current version.

Because saved patterns are stored locally on your device, access to that data depends on the security of your device. Users should protect their device with appropriate security settings.

Children’s Privacy

Pulse Studio is not intended for children. The app should only be used by users who are old enough to use apps of this type safely and responsibly.

Safety Notice

Pulse Studio uses your phone’s built-in vibration motor. Stop use if you feel discomfort, irritation, numbness, pain, unusual sensitivity, or if your device becomes warm.

Pulse Studio is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the effective date at the top of this policy will be updated.
